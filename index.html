A Cup Of...

Is a cup packed with a nutritious and delicious meal to enjoy on your next adventure. We emphasize on quality organic ingredients and flavorful experiences which leaves the stomach in balance with the outdoor life.

They are without additives, high in protein and biodegradable. At the bottom of the cup - in its cavity - are added seeds, which bloom wildly when the cups have been broken down by nature's own cycle.

Follow the guide down below to make sure you give the best circumstances for the seeds to grow.

We care from the beginning to the end.

That's right. Our cups are biodegradable.

Biodegradable-what?

Taking care of our planet is one of the most important conversations in the world right now. We made sure the materials of our meal cups will always be able to decompose in nature’s open, wild and beautiful surroundings.

The cup only leaves seeds behind to grow wild and free, just like your hiking spirit. Average decomposing time is set between 4-6 weeks depending on the location.

So yes, this means you can leave the cup behind you without any guilt. The future of packaging is here, we must think how the product's end should be handled. We can't wait to bring you along the journey, as much as you bring us along yours!
